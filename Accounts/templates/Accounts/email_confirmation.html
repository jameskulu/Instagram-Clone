Didn't get an email?

If you don't see an email from us within a few minutes, a few things could have happened:

  • The email is in your spam folder. (Sometimes things get lost in there.) The email address you entered had a mistake or typo. (Happens to the best of us.)
  • You accidentally gave us another email address. (Usually a work or personal one you meant.)
  • We can't deliver the email to this address. (Usually because of corporate firewalls or filtering)
